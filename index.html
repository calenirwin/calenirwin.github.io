Current Work
Research

I am a member of the Big Data Analytics and Management Laboratory (BAM Lab) at Queen's. Under the supervision of Dr. Farhana Zulkernine, I am completing my thesis on unsupervised anomaly detection in sensor timeseries data. Specifically I am interested in developing models and approaches that have business value in the context of large-scale, unlabeled data where anomalous behaviour is not well characterized.
